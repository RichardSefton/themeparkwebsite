By Bus

  • Guests may take bus number 968 to VivoCity station and trasit to Main entrance of USS via tram. There you can buy tickets at the 16 Wonders attractions' entrance.
  • Bus 968 runs from 7:00 a.m. to 11:30 p.m. everyday.

By Train

  • Take the Green Line to Sentosa station and transit to Orange line at Imbiah lookout.
  • From there you will reach the entrance to 16 Wonders attractions. You can buy tickets at the entrance.
  • Green Line runs from 7:00 a.m. to 12:00 a.m. everyday
    •
  • Orange Line runs from 7:00 a.m. to 10:30 p.m. everyday

By Personal Vehicle

  • From the west - Drive along CTE highway until Harbourfront exit.
  • From there, take the right towards RWS way and you will reach the entrance to the 16 wonders attractions.

  • From the east - Drive along the Keppel Terminal way and turn at the CTE high way.
  • Upon reaching Harbourfront exit, turn left towards RWS way and you will reach the entrance to the 16 wonders attractions.

Special needs access

Guests with any type of disability can have a great time at the 16 Wonders. Assistance for special needs is offered from the park's Guest Service centers, found near both the South and the North Entrance.

Are car parks have disabled parking and ramps lifts are provided as an alternative to starts. If you have a carer they can also access the park free of charge!
