How to get to us

Bus

Bus No. From
AB-01 Downtown
BB-12 City B

Train

  • Take the Blue Line and get off at Theme Park #16 Station for direct access to park south entrance
  • Take the Green Line and get off at King Station for 5 minutes walk to park north entrance

Car

  • Drive through the Northern Tunnel from Downtown via the no.11 highway. The tunnel fee is US$5(one-way). After the tunnel, you will be on King Road. Turn right into Amusement Park #16 to reach the North Entrance.
  • Drive through the Sourthern Bridge from Harbour via the Queen Road. Turn Right to Ewdard Road and turn left to the Amusement Park Road to reach the South Entrance.

Where to sleep

  • PLAYHOUSE

    This playhouse inspired hotel, is a dream come true for your children. Equipped with numerous slides around the hotel and jungle gyms in various places, this is the place for them. Enjoy an all-you-can-eat buffet experience, and a chocolate fountain perfect for breakfast or dessert.

  • OAKWOOD LODGE

    Enjoy the expereince of being in your own cabin, right here on the park grounds. Get the authentic lodge lifestyle with real fireplaces, and an oak scent throughout the lodge.

  • THE BELLEVIEW

    This all inclusive hotel ensures that your stay is easy, and stress free. Enjoy an amazing view of the park's grounds from any floor. Go for a dip in the pool, for a quiet serene swim, exclusive to this hotel.

Where to eat

  • LA CREME BRULEE

    Located in The Belleview, this restaurant brings top cuisine straight to your plate. Enjoy our extensive line of pinots and reds from our coveted wine bar.

  • UNDER THE SEA

    Located in Playhouse, this restaurant brings seafood to the table. Dine with Nemo and his friends in this kid-friendly dining hall.

  • THE PINE

    Located in Oakwood Lodge, this restaurant is known for its wood oven pizza and smoked salmon. Enjoy the naturesque themes of this dining establishment.

Special needs access

Guests with any type of disability can have a great time at Amusement Park #16. Assistance for special needs is offered from the park's Guest Service centers, found near both the South and the North Entrance.
