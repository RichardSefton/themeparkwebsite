How to get to us

Bus

Guests may take any 'A' or 'C' bus routes to the 16 Wonders, which runs regularly during the day back and forth to downtown.

Route: A, C from 6:00 AM to 12:05 AM everyday

Train

Take the Blue Line and get off at the 16 Wonders Station for direct access to park south entrance

Take the Green Line and get off at King Station for 5 minutes walk to park north entrance

Car

Drive through the Northern Tunnel from Downtown via the no.11 highway. The tunnel fee is US$5(one-way). After the tunnel, you will be on King Road. Turn right into the 16 Wonders to reach the North Entrance.

Drive through the Sourthern Bridge from Harbour via the Queen Road. Turn Right to Ewdard Road and turn left to the Amusement Park Road to reach the South Entrance.

Special needs access

Guests with any type of disability can have a great time at the 16 Wonders. Assistance for special needs is offered from the park's Guest Service centers, found near both the South and the North Entrance.
